Ontario rule highlights

Key ‚Äúgotchas‚Äù students often miss

Witnessing
Most Wills must be signed by the testator in front of two witnesses present at the same time, and the witnesses must sign in the testator‚Äôs presence.
Beneficiary-witness
If a beneficiary (or a beneficiary‚Äôs spouse) witnesses your Will, their gift may be void. Pick independent witnesses whenever possible.
Holograph Will
A holograph Will can be valid in Ontario if it is wholly handwritten by the testator and signed‚Äîno witnesses required.
Remote signing
Ontario law permits remote witnessing via audio-visual technology in certain cases (typically requiring one witness to be an Ontario lawyer or paralegal).
Life updates
Marriage no longer automatically revokes a Will for marriages on/after Jan 1, 2022. Separation and divorce can still change how spousal gifts/appointments are treated‚Äîreview your Will after major life events.
